About Bright Daisies

Creating bright futures for little minds in Harare, Zimbabwe.

Who We Are

Bright Daisies Preschool is a nurturing learning environment where children aged 2-6 are encouraged to grow, explore, and shine. Located in Harare, our academy blends structured learning with creative play, helping children build confidence, independence, and early academic skills.

Founded by experienced educators, we believe that early childhood is a crucial stage in a child's development. We are committed to providing holistic education through innovative teaching methods, engaging environments, and a strong partnership with parents.

Our Mission

To inspire, educate, and empower young children by cultivating curiosity, confidence, and creativity through early childhood learning experiences.

Our Vision

To become Harare’s leading preschool, known for excellence in early education, community engagement, and child-centered learning environments.

Why Choose Us

Qualified Teachers

Our experienced and passionate teachers are trained to nurture your child’s growth and development.

Creative Curriculum

We use a blend of Montessori and play-based learning tailored for early learners in Zimbabwe.

Safe & Secure

Our campus is fully secure with child-friendly facilities and health-conscious practices.
