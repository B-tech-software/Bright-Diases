Our Fun & Educational Programs

Bright Daisies offers exciting programs full of learning and laughter!

Early Learners (Ages 2-3)

Our youngest children are introduced to playful learning through storytelling, sensory play, and movement.

Activities include: puzzles, paint play, soft toys, music circles.

Pre-K Explorers (Ages 4-5)

Designed for curious young minds to explore language, math, and art through structured play and fun group activities.

Activities include: basic counting, story creation, group games, arts & crafts.

Awesome Play Area

Kids enjoy exciting outdoor fun with jumping castles, slides, see-saws, and obstacle courses in a safe and secure environment. Perfect for building strength, confidence, and friendships!
